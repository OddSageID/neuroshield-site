A Sanctuary for Building the Shield

What the Order Offers

Not a job. A vocation.

Not escape. Purpose.

Not comfort. Dignity.

Protected Employment

The Problem You've Faced

Corporate employment ties your livelihood to shareholder interests. When those interests conflict with ethics, you lose.

The Order's Solution

Employment within faith-sponsored institution, governed by ethical principles, not profit margins.

  • Immunity from corporate retaliation: You cannot be fired for prioritizing ethics
  • Institutional backing: Vatican, LDS, Orthodox, Protestant support
  • Autonomous technical decision-making: Build what's right, not what's profitable
  • Long-term stability: Multi-year commitments, not at-will termination

You are hired to build the shield. Your job security depends on building it well.

Financial Liberation

The Problem You've Faced

Student debt becomes bondage, forcing you to accept work that violates your principles.

The Order's Solution

Debt relief in exchange for committed service building ethical systems.

How It Works

  • Years 1–2: $40,000/year student debt relief + competitive salary
  • Years 3–5: $30,000/year student debt relief + salary increases
  • Average total relief: $175,000 over 5 years
  • Salary range: $85,000–$120,000 depending on experience and location

The Math

Outside the Order: $150K debt + compromised work = cage

Within the Order: Debt eliminated + meaningful work = shield

Your financial burden becomes liberation, not coercion.

Meaningful Work

What You'll Build

1. Adaptive Cybersecurity Systems

  • Machine learning-based anomaly detection
  • Behavioral baseline analysis
  • Protection against zero-day exploits
  • Human-augmented threat response

Your Impact: Faith institutions and vulnerable populations protected from automated attacks

2. Transparent Healthcare AI

  • Authorization systems with mandatory human review
  • Bias detection and correction
  • Patient-centered algorithms
  • Explainable AI (XAI) integration

Your Impact: Prevent automated denial-at-scale; ensure technology serves healing

3. Privacy-by-Design Infrastructure

  • Network neutrality enforcement tools
  • Deep packet inspection transparency
  • Cognitive liberty protection systems
  • User consent verification

Your Impact: Protect human autonomy at the infrastructure level

4. Next-Generation Ethical AI

  • Research on human-augmented systems
  • Governance framework development
  • Open-source tool creation
  • Industry standard-setting

Your Impact: Shape the future of AI toward human flourishing

Aligned Mission

The Problem You've Faced

Your personal values conflict with corporate objectives. You experience constant moral compromise.

The Order's Solution

Your work directly implements the Rome Call for AI Ethics principles.

The Rome Call Principles You'll Operationalize

  • Transparency: Every system auditable and explainable
  • Inclusion: Bias detection prioritizes vulnerable populations
  • Responsibility: Human oversight mandatory
  • Impartiality: Fairness metrics built into design
  • Reliability: Continuous testing and improvement
  • Security: Protection without surveillance
  • Privacy: Data minimization and user control

Your technical expertise becomes the implementation of global ethical consensus.

Community and Support

The Problem You've Faced

Isolation. You've been the lone voice raising concerns. Others stayed silent. You were terminated alone.

The Order's Solution

A community of technologists who share your values.

  • Peer collaboration: Work alongside others who refused the cage
  • Mentorship: Senior technologists who've navigated these challenges
  • Professional development: Conferences, research, publication
  • Psychological support: Counseling for those recovering from corporate trauma
  • Faith integration: Optional spiritual direction and community

You are not alone anymore.

Who We're Seeking

Technical Qualifications

  • Degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field (or equivalent experience)
  • Experience with:
    • Machine learning / AI systems
    • Cybersecurity architecture
    • Software development
    • Data analysis and modeling
  • Demonstrated technical competence

Ethical Qualifications

  • Track record of prioritizing ethics over expedience
  • Evidence of:
    • Whistleblowing or internal advocacy
    • Departure from roles due to ethical concerns
    • Refusal to implement problematic systems
    • Advocacy for transparency and accountability

Values Alignment

  • Commitment to human dignity
  • Belief that technology should serve flourishing
  • Willingness to work within faith-sponsored framework (religious belief not required)
  • Long-term dedication to ethical AI

What We're Not

  • A non-profit with subsistence wages (competitive market-rate salaries)
  • A religious conversion program (faith tradition not required for membership)
  • A permanent commitment (5-year terms, renewable or concluding)
  • A shelter for underperformers (high technical standards maintained)
  • An ideological bubble (diverse perspectives valued within ethical framework)

This IS:

  • Protected employment for building ethical systems
  • Financial liberation from debt coercion
  • Meaningful work aligned with human dignity
  • Community of principled technologists
  • Pathway to influence the future of AI
