Accessibility Statement

Last Updated: November 2025

The Order of Ethical Technologists is committed to practicing the ethical principles we advocate. These policies reflect our foundational belief that technology must serve human dignity, autonomy, and flourishing.

The Order of Ethical Technologists is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for all individuals, including those with disabilities. We strive to follow the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 at the AA level.

Our Commitment: Digital accessibility is not merely compliance—it is a moral imperative. Technology must serve all humans, especially those whom traditional interfaces exclude.

Measures We Are Taking

We work to:

  • Provide clear, structured navigation
  • Use readable fonts and high-contrast color palettes
  • Ensure that images contain descriptive alt-text
  • Avoid flashing elements or disruptive animations
  • Maintain compatibility with screen readers
  • Ensure forms are fully keyboard-navigable

Limitations

Some content is hosted or rendered through Google Sites, which may impose certain accessibility constraints. We continuously review these limitations and seek alternatives where necessary.

Feedback

If you encounter any accessibility barriers, please contact us at:

Email: accessibility@orderoftechnologists.org

We will respond and take corrective action wherever possible.
