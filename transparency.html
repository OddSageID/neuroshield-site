Transparency Report

Last Updated: November 2025

The Order of Ethical Technologists is committed to practicing the ethical principles we advocate. These policies reflect our foundational belief that technology must serve human dignity, autonomy, and flourishing.

The Order of Ethical Technologists seeks to maintain a high standard of transparency in all operations, consistent with our mission of ethical stewardship.

1. Governance Transparency

At this stage:

  • Leadership is provisional
  • Advisors are disclosed publicly upon confirmation
  • Sponsor institutions are listed upon formal commitment
  • All governance structures will be published once finalized

2. Financial Transparency

As an emerging interinstitutional initiative:

  • Sponsorship tiers are published openly
  • Funding sources will be disclosed as they become active
  • Annual financial summaries will be published once sponsorship begins

3. Data & Privacy Transparency

We commit to:

  • Publishing major updates to the Privacy Policy
  • Disclosing any data-access requests (if ever applicable)
  • Maintaining no advertising, no tracking, and no data sales
  • Using only essential, minimal analytics tools

4. Operational Transparency

We will publish:

  • Annual progress reports
  • Milestone achievements
  • Governance changes
  • System architecture summaries (non-sensitive)

5. Ethical Commitment

Our purpose is to create AI systems and governance structures guided by ethical, interfaith, and human-centered values. Transparency is a core principle of that mission.
