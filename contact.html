Contact and Next Steps

Ready to Move Forward?

For Potential Sponsors

Schedule a Consultation

Reach out to discuss how your institution can partner with the Order:

  • 30-minute introductory call
  • Detailed proposal packet
  • Budget customization

Contact:

Email: sponsors@orderoftechnologists.org

What to Include:

  • Name and Institution
  • Role/Title
  • Institution Type (Catholic/LDS/Orthodox/Protestant/Other)
  • Preferred contact method
  • Available times for call
  • Specific questions or interests

Request Materials

Available upon request:

  • Full Sponsorship Prospectus (PDF)
  • Budget and Financial Projections (PDF)
  • Governance Structure Document (PDF)
  • Technical Specifications (PDF)

For Potential Recruits

Express Interest

Not an application—just a conversation.

Contact:

Email: recruits@orderoftechnologists.org

What to Include:

  • Name and Current Location
  • Technical Background (brief)
  • What led to your departure from previous role
  • Areas of expertise and interest
  • Student debt level (approximate)
  • Preferred contact method
  • Any questions

Timeline: We respond within one week to schedule a call.

Request Materials

Available upon request:

  • Recruit Information Packet (PDF)
  • Application Requirements (PDF)
  • Debt Relief Program Details (PDF)
  • FAQ Extended Version (PDF)
