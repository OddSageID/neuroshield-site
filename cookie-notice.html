Cookie Notice

Last Updated: March 2025

The Order of Ethical Technologists is committed to practicing the ethical principles we advocate. These policies reflect our foundational belief that technology must serve human dignity, autonomy, and flourishing.

The Order of Ethical Technologists ("the Order," "we," "our") uses cookies only in the limited ways necessary to ensure basic functionality and security of this website. We do not use cookies for advertising, behavioral profiling, cross-site tracking, or commercial analytics.

This Cookie Notice explains what cookies may be used on this site and how you can manage them.

Cookie Usage Summary

Analytics No We do not use analytics cookies for behavioral tracking
Advertising / Marketing No We do not serve advertisements or use marketing cookies

Educational Note for Recruits

The Order follows a minimal data philosophy: Only what is required for function, security, and ethical integrity is allowed.

This table exists to help technologists, clergy, and institutional leaders understand why the site behaves the way it does. It also demonstrates how ethical AI governance begins with simple, transparent digital practices.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites you visit. They can help a site:

  • Function properly
  • Remember basic settings
  • Improve stability and performance

This site uses minimal, functional-only cookies.

2. How We Use Cookies

Because our website is hosted on Google Sites, the platform may automatically set a small number of essential cookies required for:

  • Security
  • Load balancing
  • Preventing malicious activity
  • Basic site functionality

These cookies:

  • Do not track behavior
  • Do not identify you personally
  • Do not follow you across other websites
  • Do not store sensitive information

We do not:

  • Serve advertisements
  • Use analytics cookies for behavioral data
  • Use marketing cookies
  • Sell or share cookie data

3. Types of Cookies Used

Essential / Strictly Necessary Cookies

These are required for the site to function. They may manage:

  • Page rendering
  • Form submission stability
  • Security features
  • Load balancing for Google hosting

You cannot disable these without affecting the site's basic operation.

No Non-Essential Cookies

We do not use:

  • Analytics cookies (Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, etc.)
  • Marketing cookies
  • Personalization or profiling cookies

4. Managing Cookies

Because this site uses only essential cookies, most cookie-management tools in your browser will categorize our cookies as "necessary."

However, you may still manage or delete cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome
  • Firefox
  • Edge
  • Safari
  • Opera

Turning off all cookies may cause certain pages to function incorrectly, particularly forms.

5. Changes to This Notice

We may update this Cookie Notice if the website introduces new features or if Google Sites changes its default cookie configuration. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.

6. Contact

If you have questions about cookies or online privacy, contact:

Email: privacy@orderoftechnologists.org
